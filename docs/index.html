The world's best framework for Honeypot

Quick Start

Several modes

Sensor, Low-to-High interaction, standalone server.

With several modes available, you can customize HoneyTrap so it adapts to your needs and your threats.

Easy service deployment

As many services as you want.

Adding new services is as easy as implementing one method. All the rest is being done by the HoneyTrap framework.

Central Management

Decentralized agents.

Using HoneyTrap Agents you can deploy many agents across your organization and connect them to an unique central HoneyTrap server.

Advanced logging system

Choose your own.

The advanced logging system enables you to log HoneyTrap interactions to all kind of destinations, like Elasticsearch, Splunk, Raven Server, Slack, Kafka, or console and files.
